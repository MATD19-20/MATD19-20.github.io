What is this? ü§î

We are multiple masters students at the University of Reading: the MATD 2019/2020 class. Under normal circumstances we‚Äôd spend our waking hours working together at the studio. However, due to the coronavirus pandemic we are confined to our homes. So, what are a bunch of type designers to do but start a daily design challenge?
How it works:
We each take turns providing a daily file with the basic structure and a pre-defined number of nodes and components to keep the masters compatible. The individual files of the chosen letter are combined at the end of the day to create a variable font.
